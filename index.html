My name is Lathika Kathiresan. I love programming and am currently doing my undergraduate in Computer Science at Dalhousie University. I firmly believe that is it our duty as tech designers to ensure that technology is accessible and safe to all, and so I've put together my own writings and some external resources on some of the social and ethical issues in computer science so that everyone can understand the intricacies of the technology around them regardless of their technical knowledge.
